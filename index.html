Hey! I'm Bhavesh Korane, a B.Tech Computer Science Engineer from Kolhapur, currently working as a Software Engineer (L1) at Tekdi Technologies, Pune. I focus on backend development and AI integration, building scalable systems using Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Docker, Redis, and Streamlit.
